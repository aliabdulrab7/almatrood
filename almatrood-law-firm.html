About the Firm

Defending Rights. Delivering Justice.

Al Matrood Law Firm is one of Saudi Arabia's distinguished legal practices, headquartered in Riyadh. Licensed by the Saudi Ministry of Justice and the Saudi Bar Association, our firm combines deep knowledge of Saudi legal frameworks with an understanding of international business law.

We represent individuals, corporations, and institutions across a wide spectrum of legal matters — always guided by the principles of integrity, professionalism, and results. Our attorneys are not just legal advisors; they are strategic partners committed to your success.
