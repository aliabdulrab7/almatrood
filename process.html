How We Work

From your first consultation to the resolution of your case, we follow a rigorous, transparent process designed to deliver the best possible outcome — keeping you informed every step of the way.

Step by Step

Our 6-Step Legal Process

Every client and every case is unique — but our approach is consistent: careful, strategic, and always client-focused. Here is exactly how we work from the moment you reach out to us until your matter is fully resolved.

01
Initial Contact

Free Consultation & Case Assessment

Your journey with us begins with a free, no-obligation consultation — available in person at our Riyadh office, by phone, or via video call. During this session, one of our senior attorneys will listen carefully to your situation, ask clarifying questions, and give you an honest, initial assessment of your legal position and options. There is no pressure and no commitment.

🕐
Duration
60–90 minute initial session, with follow-up if needed
📍
Format
In-person (Riyadh), phone, or secure video call
💬
Language
Available fully in Arabic and English
02
Engagement

Scope, Fees & Engagement Agreement

Once you decide to proceed, we provide a clear, written engagement letter outlining the scope of our work, the assigned attorney, the fee structure, and expected timelines. We believe complete transparency about costs eliminates surprises and builds trust from the very beginning. You'll know exactly what you're paying for and when.

📄
Deliverable
Written engagement letter and signed service agreement
💰
Fees
Transparent fixed fees or retainer — agreed upfront
👤
Assignment
Dedicated attorney matched to your practice area
03
Investigation

Deep-Dive Research & Evidence Gathering

Your assigned attorney conducts thorough legal research, reviews all documentation, and gathers supporting evidence. For litigation cases, this includes examining court records, interviewing witnesses, obtaining expert opinions, and preparing a comprehensive case file. We leave no stone unturned — because winning cases is built on preparation, not luck.

🔍
Research
Saudi statutes, precedent cases, regulatory codes
📂
Documentation
Comprehensive case file and evidence catalogue
🧑‍🔬
Experts
Access to forensic, medical, and financial experts
04
Strategy

Legal Strategy Development & Client Briefing

Based on our research, we develop a tailored legal strategy with a clear primary approach and contingency plans. We then present this strategy to you in a detailed briefing — explaining the legal landscape, your realistic options, the risks involved in each path, and our recommendation. You remain in control of every major decision.

♟️
Strategy
Primary approach plus alternative scenarios
📊
Risk Analysis
Honest assessment of likelihood and outcomes
✅
Client Approval
Your consent before any action is taken
05
Execution

Legal Action, Representation & Negotiation

We execute the agreed strategy with precision — filing legal documents, representing you in court hearings, conducting negotiations, and managing all communications with opposing parties, courts, and government authorities. You receive regular progress updates so you are never left wondering what is happening with your case.

🏛️
Court Work
All filings, appearances, and oral arguments handled
🤝
Negotiation
Settlement negotiations when in your best interest
📲
Updates
Regular progress reports — no radio silence
06
Resolution

Case Resolution & Post-Matter Support

Once your case is resolved — whether through judgment, settlement, or administrative decision — we handle all necessary follow-up actions: enforcing judgments, completing registrations, or filing appeals if required. We also conduct a case debrief with you and remain available as your long-term legal partner for any future matters that arise.

🏆
Resolution
Judgment enforcement, settlement finalization
📋
Documentation
Final case report and all original documents returned
🔄
Ongoing
Available as your long-term legal partner
