Founder & Managing Partner · Business & Corporate Law

Abdullah Al Matrood is the founder and Managing Partner of Al Matrood Law Firm, with over 20 years of distinguished legal practice in Saudi Arabia. His career spans the full spectrum of corporate and commercial law — from founding-stage business structuring to complex multinational transactions and high-stakes commercial litigation.

A graduate of King Saud University Faculty of Law with a postgraduate diploma in International Commercial Law, Abdullah has represented some of the Kingdom's leading corporations, government agencies, and foreign investors. He is known for his strategic acumen, meticulous preparation, and ability to navigate the intersection of Saudi, Islamic, and international law.

Abdullah is a registered member of the Saudi Bar Association and a licensed practitioner before all courts of the Kingdom, including the Supreme Court. He regularly advises boards on corporate governance and speaks at regional legal conferences on Vision 2030 legal reforms.
