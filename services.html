Business & Corporate Law

Commercial Excellence
Get Advice →

Whether you're launching a startup, managing a growing enterprise, or navigating complex international business transactions, Al Matrood Law Firm provides the comprehensive corporate legal support you need. We understand the Saudi regulatory landscape inside-out and work proactively to protect your commercial interests at every turn.

Company Formation
End-to-end support for incorporating businesses in Saudi Arabia, including LLC, joint-stock, and branch office registrations with MISA.
Contract Drafting
Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating commercial contracts, joint ventures, shareholder agreements, and MoUs that protect your interests.
Mergers & Acquisitions
Legal due diligence, deal structuring, and transaction management for mergers, acquisitions, and business sales in the Kingdom.
Regulatory Compliance
Guidance on Vision 2030 reforms, ZATCA regulations, SAMA licensing, and sector-specific compliance requirements.
Dispute Resolution
Representation in commercial disputes before Saudi courts, the SCCA, and international arbitration bodies.
Employment Law
Advising on Saudi Labor Law, employment contracts, Saudization (Nitaqat), and workforce restructuring matters.

Family Law

Compassionate Counsel
Get Advice →

Family legal matters require not only legal expertise but also sensitivity, discretion, and genuine care. Our family law attorneys handle every case with compassion, guided by both Islamic principles and Saudi Personal Status Law. We work to achieve the best possible outcomes for you and your family while minimizing conflict and stress.

Marriage Contracts
Drafting and reviewing marriage contracts, prenuptial agreements, and mahr arrangements in accordance with Islamic and Saudi law.
Divorce Proceedings
Representing clients in all types of divorce — talaq, khul', faskh — and negotiating favorable terms for settlement and division of assets.
Child Custody
Fighting for the best interests of your children in custody, visitation rights, and guardianship proceedings before Saudi family courts.
Inheritance & Wills
Estate planning, will drafting, and resolving inheritance disputes in accordance with Islamic inheritance law (fara'id).
Alimony & Support
Legal advice and representation on nafaqa (spousal support), child maintenance, and financial settlements post-divorce.
Guardianship
Assistance with legal guardianship applications, wali al-amr appointments, and related documentation for minors and dependents.

Criminal Defense

Vigorous Advocacy
Get Advice →

When your freedom is at stake, you need experienced, fearless legal representation. Our criminal defense team has appeared before Saudi criminal courts at all levels — from investigative hearings to appellate tribunals. We are dedicated to protecting your rights at every stage of the process, ensuring due process is upheld.

Pre-Arrest Advice
Immediate legal guidance if you are under investigation, including advice on your rights during questioning and police interviews.
Bail Applications
Urgent applications for release, bail conditions, and detainee rights before the Public Prosecution and criminal courts.
Trial Representation
Expert courtroom advocacy in criminal trials covering financial crimes, fraud, assault, drug offenses, and cybercrime.
Appeals
Filing and arguing appeals before the Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court to challenge convictions or reduce sentences.
White-Collar Crime
Specialized defense for corporate fraud, embezzlement, money laundering, bribery, and economic crimes under Saudi law.
Victim Representation
Representing crime victims in pursuing criminal complaints, qisas (retribution), and diyya (blood money) claims in Saudi courts.

Personal Injury

Fighting for Compensation
Get Advice →

Suffering an injury due to someone else's negligence is devastating. Our personal injury attorneys pursue maximum compensation on your behalf — covering medical expenses, lost income, and the pain and suffering you've endured. We work on a results-focused basis, ensuring accountability for those responsible.

Traffic Accidents
Claims against negligent drivers, insurance companies, and transport operators for road traffic accident injuries and fatalities.
Workplace Injuries
Pursuing GOSI claims and employer liability cases for workplace accidents, occupational diseases, and unsafe working conditions.
Medical Negligence
Complex claims against hospitals, clinics, and healthcare professionals for misdiagnosis, surgical errors, and substandard care.
Slip & Fall
Premises liability claims against property owners, businesses, and municipalities for injuries caused by dangerous conditions.
Product Liability
Claims against manufacturers and distributors of defective products that have caused injury or financial harm to consumers.
Insurance Disputes
Representing policyholders in disputes with insurance companies over denied or underpaid injury and liability claims.
